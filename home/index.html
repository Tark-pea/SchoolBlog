Get Help

If you or someone you know is experiencing bullying, please reach out for help. Here are some resources that can provide support:

  • National Bullying Prevention Hotline: 1-800-420-1479
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741
